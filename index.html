Domestic Goat

The Goat is one of the animals in the Chinese zodiac. Those born in the year of the goat are predicted to be shy, introverted, creative, and perfectionist.

  • Trinomial Name: Capra aegarus hircus
  • Personality: Curious
  • Domestication Date: About 10,000 years BP
  • Did you know? Goats have prominent split pupils!

Goats were domesticated for their multiple uses and providable resourcres, as well as their relative cost of care being cheaper than that of cattle. These include milk, meat, cheese, hide, manure, and even more!
